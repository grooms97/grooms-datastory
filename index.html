My Personal Data Story

Data Collection

Welcome to my site! I tracked my Twitter data for 5 days in order to learn about the correlation between my Twitter usage and mood. Check out my findings in the material below. When exploring Twitter during week I payed special attention to:

  • The time
  • My intention
  • My mood
  • The device I was using (i.e. iPhone or laptop)
  • My location
  • The amount of time spent
  • The account handle (i.e. @NBCNews)
  • The account status (i.e. verified)
  • Whether or not I was following the account
  • The media (i.e. video, image)
  • The topic
  • The subject
  • Tweet interaction (i.e. 250 retweets)
